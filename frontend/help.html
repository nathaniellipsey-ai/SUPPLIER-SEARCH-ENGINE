Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does the AI Score mean?
A: The AI Score (0-100) indicates how well a supplier matches your criteria based on their profile, ratings, and products offered. Higher scores indicate better matches.
Q: Can I export the supplier list?
A: Yes! Click the "EXPORT" button in the toolbar to download filtered results as a CSV file.
Q: How do I compare suppliers?
A: Click the "COMPARE (0)" button to enter comparison mode. Select suppliers using checkboxes, then click the button again to view the comparison table.
Q: Where are my favorites and notes saved?
A: They are saved in your browser's local storage. They persist across sessions as long as you use the same browser.
Q: What does "Walmart Verified" mean?
A: Walmart Verified suppliers have been verified and approved by Walmart's supplier verification team, indicating they meet our quality and compliance standards.
Q: How often is the supplier data updated?
A: Data is updated regularly as new suppliers are added and existing supplier information is verified.
Q: Can I print the supplier information?
A: Yes! Click the "PRINT" button in the toolbar to print the current filtered results.
